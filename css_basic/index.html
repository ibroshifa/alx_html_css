Falling in Love with the Problem:

Embrace the Challenge

Shift Perspective: Instead of seeing problems as roadblocks, view them as opportunities to create positive change.

Curiosity is Key: Approach problems with an inquisitive mind. Ask "why" and "how" to truly understand the root cause.

Deep Dive & Understanding:

Research & Empathy: Immerse yourself in the problem. Talk to people affected, gather data, and understand the context.

Define the Scope: Clearly define the problem you're solving. What are its boundaries and who are the stakeholders?
