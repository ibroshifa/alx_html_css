Falling in Love with the Problem: Embrace the Challenge

Focus on understanding the problem: Rather than jumping to solutions, prioritize deeply understanding the issue you're facing. This involves gathering information, analyzing different aspects, and empathizing with those affected.

Curiosity is Key: Approach problems with an inquisitive mind. Ask "why" and "how" to truly understand the root cause.

see challenges as opportunities: Challenges can be a chance to learn, grow, and innovate. When you view them as puzzles to solve, it can spark creativity and a sense of purpose.

Enjoy the process: Finding the solution can be just as rewarding as achieving the end goal. Embrace the journey of tackling the problem, celebrate small wins, and find satisfaction in the intellectual effort.

Avoid solution bias: Don't get fixated on a particular solution too early. Falling in love with the problem allows you to explore various approaches and find the most effective one.

Research & Empathy: Immerse yourself in the problem. Talk to people affected, gather data, and understand the context.

Define the Scope: Clearly define the problem you're solving. What are its boundaries and who are the stakeholders?
